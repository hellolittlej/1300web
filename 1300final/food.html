    Meet Apple Growers

    Apple Mill Orchard is located on the southern slopes of the mountains of Western North Carolina, home of apples known countrywide for exceptional flavor. Apple Mill offers many products during the Festival including many types of fresh apples from our orchard. You can also purchase apple butters, jams, and preserves during the Festival or at their store which is open year round in Saluda, NC.

    Drake Farms

    Mark Drake and his family have been apple growers in Henderson County for many years. They have been a part of the Apple Festival since 2012. They offer many varieties of fresh apples including Honey Crisp, Galas, and many others during the festival. You can also buy apple sippers, apple slushies, and caramel apples from their booth. Mark and his family invite you to stop by his booth and enjoy a North Carolina Apple.
